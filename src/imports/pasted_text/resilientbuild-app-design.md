Design a complete mobile-first application UI/UX called:

# ResilientBuild Pakistan

Tagline:
"Choose Location. Build Safe."

Purpose:
A national resilient construction advisory platform that helps citizens, builders, engineers, contractors, local governments, and disaster management agencies identify the safest house models for any location in Pakistan and learn exactly how to construct them through interactive engineering animations, 3D visualizations, and step-by-step construction guidance.

The application should look modern, premium, highly professional, government-grade, and inspired by Google Maps, Autodesk Construction Cloud, ArcGIS, Tesla UI, and Duolingo-style learning experiences.

Color Theme:

* Deep Navy Blue
* Disaster Management Orange
* Infrastructure Steel Gray
* White backgrounds
* Success Green
* Hazard Red
* Modern glassmorphism cards
* Soft shadows
* Clean typography
* Minimalist engineering style

Design System:

* Mobile-first
* Android and iOS compatible
* 8px spacing grid
* Rounded corners 16px
* Professional icons
* GIS and infrastructure themed illustrations
* Modern micro-interactions
* Dark mode support

Create all screens and user flows.
